A Final Year Project: Bridging the Technology Gap.

We are a dedicated group of **five B.Tech final-year students** focused on solving real-world agricultural problems. In India, many farmers still face significant crop loss because they cannot quickly or accurately identify diseases.

**AgriShield** is our solution. Our goal is to provide a simple, powerful tool that requires no technical expertise. By replacing manual inspection with **instant AI detection**, we empower farmers to take timely action, ensuring healthier crops and better yields. This project is our commitment to the future of sustainable Indian agriculture.

How AgriShield Works

1. Capture & Upload

Use a smartphone to take a clear photo of the infected leaf and upload it to the site.

2. AI Diagnosis

Our trained deep learning model instantly analyzes the image for known disease patterns.

3. Get Solutions

Receive the name of the disease, confidence score, and tailored scientific and organic treatment plans.
